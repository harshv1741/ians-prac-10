Chinese Remainder Theorem



ALGORITHM



Step1: Find M1=m1*m2*m3*...*mk.This is the common modulus.


Step2: Find M1=M/m1,M2=M/m2,...,Mk=M/mk.


Step3: Find the multiplicative inverse of M1,M2,...,MK using the corresponding moduli (m1,m2,...,mk).Call the inverse is: M1-1,M2-1,...,Mk-1.


Step4: The solution to the simultaneous equation is X=(a1*M1*M1-1+a2*M2*M2-1+...+ak*Mk*Mk-1)mod M.
